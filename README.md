# Life Canvas - Your Personal Digital Journal

Life Canvas is a secure and personal daily journal designed to help you write down your thoughts, track your mood, and reflect on your day.

## ‚ú® Features

-   **Secure User Authentication:** Sign up and log in securely with email and password.
-   **Rich Journaling:** Create, edit, and delete journal entries with a title, content, date, and mood.
-   **Mood Tracking:** Assign one of five moods (Happy, Calm, Neutral, Energetic, Sad) to each entry.
-   **AI-Powered Reflections:** Get gentle, encouraging reflections on your writing, powered by Google's Gemini API.
-   **Calendar View:** Visualize your mood patterns over the month with an interactive D3.js calendar.
-   **Journaling Streak:** Stay motivated by tracking your consecutive days of writing.
-   **Customizable Themes:** Personalize your journal with multiple beautiful color themes (including Forest, Sunset, Ocean, and more).
-   **Daily Reminders:** Set a daily notification to remind you to write.
-   **Dark Mode:** The app respects your system's dark mode preference.
